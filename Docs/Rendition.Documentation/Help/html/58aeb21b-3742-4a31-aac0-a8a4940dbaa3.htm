Commerce applications are related to accounts, orders, items and anything else related to the buying / selling process. Just think, you're participating in one of the oldest known activities. This ties you together with ancient African tribesmen trading seeds for high occupancy time shares near the Rock of Gibraltar.

Below is a summary of the topics in this section.

Categories

Create categories, lists of items, to display to your customers or to help manage reports and purchasing.

Change Status

Change the status of an order, shipment or line.

Commerce Context Menu

Right (Mac: Command+Click) clicking on results in a grid results in a menu when the grid contains important columns, like Order Number or Account Number. Allowing quick access to these accounts, orders, etc..

General Ledger

When your order has reached the end of its lifecycle, post it to the general ledger then you can export it to an accounting system.

Inventory

Check the status of your inventory. See if it's time to put somthing on sale, or buy more.

Item Editor

Edit and create your items. Add images, properties, descriptions, pricing. Add items to categories and much more.

Make Payment

Make a payment. Create credits, pay orders, add payments to bills. Almost all monitary transactions are handled here.

Order Editor

Create and edit orders and purchase orders. Print orders and change the status of lines, shipments and orders.

Production Aging

Check the status of every item or order in your production line. Make sure unfilled orders don't get too old.

Production Picklist

Create lists of items to use in your ordering or on the floor in your production line.

Sales Journal

Get a table or chart of how much money you're making and who's making it.

Shipping

Create and edit details of shipping carriers and services. Upload rate file from your carrier and show your customers your rates in real time.

User Editor

Create and edit users, set prices, add custom printable forms, set startup options, security levels and much more.
